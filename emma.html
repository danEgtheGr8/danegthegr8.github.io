About Emma

Emma was born in 1981 at Guys Hospital, and grew up in Bermondsey, South-East London.

She is a devoted mum to two lovely children, and spends most of her spare time running round cleaning up after them, and getting their stuff ready for school.

She is currently a PA and HR officer at Redriff Primary school, and her hobbies include looking for the next thing to spend all of her money on.
