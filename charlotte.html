About Charlotte

Charlotte was born in 2017 at the Pricess Royal University Hospital in Farnborough, Kent, and has spent most of her life so far at our current home in Darenth.

She is our sassy princess, and has us in stitches most of the time.

We are very proud of the young lady she is becoming, and she is doing really well at school.
